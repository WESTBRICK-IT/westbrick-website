Experienced Board of Directors & Management Team

Board of Directors

J. Douglas Kay

Chairman

Doug Kay was the founder and CEO of two private companies, Segue Energy Corporation (2000-2003) and Intrepid Energy Corporation (2004-2009) and is currently a director of Freehold Royalties. Doug Kay is an experienced executive with strong land, finance, business and leadership skills acquired through 41 years of diverse responsibilities in primarily Canadian oil and gas exploration and production companies. Doug Kay obtained a BA(Econ.) from the University of Calgary, is a professional landman (P. Land) and is a former CAPP Governor and former EPAC chairman. He holds the ICD.D designation from the Institute of Corporate Directors.

M. Bruce Chernoff

Director

Bruce Chernoff is the founder and President of Caribou Capital Corp., Executive Chairman of Lucero Energy Corp. and Chairman of Artis Exploration. Since 2005, Bruce Chernoff has been the Chairman of Maxim Power Corp. Bruce Chernoff is a Professional Engineer with a Bachelor of Applied Science degree in Chemical Engineering from Queen’s University in (1987).

David J. Rain

Director

David Rain is a Vice President and Director of Caribou Capital Corp. He currently is a director of Lucero Energy Corp., a TSXV listed company operating in North Dakota. David Rain is a Chartered Accountant and obtained a B. Comm degree from the University of Saskatchewan in (1986).

James Riddell

Director

James Riddell is President, CEO and Director of Paramount Resources Ltd. He is a Director of Artis Exploration and Big Rock Brewery. James Riddell has a BSc. Geology from Arizona State and a MSc. Geology from the University of Alberta.

Paul Workman

Director – KKR

Paul Workman joined KKR in 2014 and is a member of the KKR North American Infrastructure team. Prior to joining KKR, he was with RBC Capital Markets in Calgary, where he focused on merger, acquisition and financing transactions in the energy industry. Prior to RBC, Paul worked in the oil and gas industry focusing on engineering. Paul Workman holds a B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering and a B.A. in Economics from Queen’s University.

Brandon Freiman

Director – KKR

Brandon A. Freiman joined KKR in 2007 and is a partner and the Head of North American Infrastructure. Prior to joining KKR, he was with Credit Suisse Securities in its energy investment banking group, where he was involved in a number of merger, acquisition, and other corporate advisory transactions. Brandon Freiman holds a B. Comm. degree with first class honors from McGill University.

Andrea Whyte

Director

Andrea Whyte has over 20 years of experience practicing law. She is a corporate partner with Osler, Hoskin & Harcourt LLP in Calgary. Andrea Whyte’s practice includes mergers and acquisitions, corporate finance, and corporate governance. She has served on boards of public and private companies as well as non-profits. Andrea Whyte obtained a B.Sc. from McGill University and an LLB from the University of Alberta (with distinction) and was appointed as King's Counsel in 2024.

Management Team

Ken McCagherty

President & CEO

Ken McCagherty is a Professional Engineer with 40+ years of experience in the Canadian & international upstream energy business. He was previously CEO & President of West Energy Ltd., a TSX listed company he founded in 2003 and sold in May 2010. Ken was a key management team member at Anderson, early Encal, HCO Energy, Petrocorp (NZ), Pacalta and Petrobank. Ken McCagherty earned a B.Sc. in Chemical Engineering from the University of Alberta in (1980) and is past Chairman of the EPAC Board of Governors.

Moe Mangat

COO

Moe Mangat was previously Vice President Exploitation at West Energy, and prior thereto, was Manager Operations at Sound Energy Trust (a public energy trust). Moe began his career at Petro Canada in 1998. Moe has a BSc. Chemical Engineering from UBC (2001) and a MEng in Project Management from University of Calgary (2007) and holds a CFA designation.

Dean Kobelka

VP Finance & CFO

Dean Kobelka was previously with Bonavista Energy Corporation where he spent the past 21 years in various roles culminating in his appointment in May 2015 as Vice President, Finance and CFO. James O'Connor

VP Land
